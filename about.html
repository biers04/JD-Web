About JD Grants & Research, LLC

Welcome to my website! My name is Jennifer Daut and I am the Owner and Grant Writer for JD Grants Hello and & Research, LLC. I graduated in (date) with a Master’s degree in Media Communications from Webster University. While obtaining my degree, I worked as a Technical Writer and Communications Analyst. Since the start of my business, I have helped non-profits obtain over $$$ in grant funds. In addition to writing grants, I also assist my clients in researching grant opportunities and updating their websites. Why does your non-profit need my services? Many non-profits do not have the staffing resources or the funds to hire a full time grant writer. And, if you are the Director of a non-profit organization, you do not have time to invest on writing grant proposals and following up on new grant opportunities. I charge an hourly fee for grant research and grant writing. Payment is due within 30 days of a grant submission, regardless of whether a grant is awarded. All grants are reviewed by key people in your organization prior to submission and all of your information is kept confidential. When I am not researching and writing grants, I am involved in the following organizations – Grant Professionals Association, Asthma and Allergy Foundation (Food allergy committee), Creative Arts Council and the Arnold, MO Chamber of Commerce. If you would like more information fill out the information on the contact page and I would be happy to speak with you!
