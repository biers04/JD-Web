About JD Grants & Research, LLC

JD Grants & Research is a foundation run by Jennifer Daut. She has been in charge of the foundation for the past 3 years, since its founding. JD Grants & Research is dedicated to helping small foundations find grants from the government and private institutions to help fund research that JD will also attempt to find.
